Legal

Key legal considerations for using DESD Marketplace.

Important Notice

This page provides general information about legal and compliance considerations for a food marketplace. It is not legal advice. Requirements vary by country/region and by the type of food products sold.

Food Safety & Product Responsibility

  • Producers are responsible for the safety, quality, labeling, and suitability of products they list.
  • Maintain appropriate handling, storage, and cold-chain practices where applicable.
  • Ensure accurate product descriptions (e.g., ingredients, allergens, origin, and best-before information when relevant).

Consumer Rights & Returns

  • Customers may have statutory consumer rights (refunds, replacements, remedies) depending on local law.
  • Perishable goods often have special rules; producers should respond promptly to quality complaints.

Platform Liability & Disclaimers

  • DESD Marketplace facilitates transactions between customers and independent producers.
  • Where permitted by law, the platform may limit liability for indirect losses and acts/omissions of third-party sellers.
  • Nothing on the platform removes or overrides mandatory legal protections.

AI Features (Recommendations & Quality Checks)

  • AI outputs are indicative and may be inaccurate; human review is recommended for low-confidence results.
  • Producers remain responsible for business decisions (pricing, stock adjustments, disposal decisions).
  • Model performance can vary by lighting, camera quality, and product variety.

Privacy & Data Handling

  • Account information and order details are used to operate the service (authentication, fulfilment, reporting).
  • Uploaded images may be stored for auditability of AI assessments and system monitoring.
  • Data retention and access should follow applicable privacy laws and organizational policies.

Intellectual Property

  • Producers should only upload photos and content they own or have permission to use.
  • Misuse of copyrighted/trademarked content may lead to removal or account action.

Regulatory Compliance

  • Some products may require licenses, inspections, or certifications depending on jurisdiction.
  • Taxes (VAT/GST/sales tax), invoicing, and reporting requirements may apply to producers.
Quick Summary

Producers are responsible for product safety and compliance. The platform provides tools (including AI) to help, but does not replace legal obligations.

For formal requirements, consult your local regulator or legal advisor.
